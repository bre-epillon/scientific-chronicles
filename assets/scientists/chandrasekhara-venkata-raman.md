Ever wonder why the sky is blue? While Raman didn't *discover* that, he did discover something equally cool with light! ✨ This brilliant Indian physicist won a Nobel for "Raman scattering." Basically, he found that when light bounces off a transparent material, it changes its frequency a bit. This might sound nerdy, but it's super important for understanding what materials are made of – like, *really* important in science and tech! Think of it as light telling us its secret. 🤫💡
