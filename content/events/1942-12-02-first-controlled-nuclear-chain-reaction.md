In a squash court under the stands of a football stadium in Chicago, Enrico Fermi and his team built a massive pile of graphite and uranium. On December 2, 1942, they achieved the first self-sustaining nuclear chain reaction. 

It was the dawn of the nuclear age. Fermi reportedly sent a coded message: 'The Italian navigator has landed in the New World.' Luckily, the 'New World' didn't involve the stadium blowing up. It was a controlled, quiet moment that changed the course of history forever. ☢️
