In 1911, Yale professor Hiram Bingham was looking for the 'lost city' of Vilcabamba when a local farmer led him up a steep mountain ridge to Machu Picchu. 

He found a stunning, intact Inca citadel that had been hidden from the Spanish for centuries. While the locals had never forgotten it, Bingham brought it to the world's attention. It remains the most iconic symbol of the Inca Empire and one of the world's greatest archaeological mysteries. Not a bad day for a hike. ⛰️
