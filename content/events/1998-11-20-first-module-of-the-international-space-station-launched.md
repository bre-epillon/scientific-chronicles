The Zarya module was the first piece of the International Space Station (ISS) to reach orbit. It was built by Russia and funded by the US, a symbol of post-Cold War cooperation in space. 

Over the next decade, more modules were added like a giant Lego set. Since November 2000, there has been a continuous human presence in space. The ISS is a floating lab, a peace treaty, and a home for humans 250 miles above the ground. It’s proof that we can build something incredible when we work together. 🛰️
