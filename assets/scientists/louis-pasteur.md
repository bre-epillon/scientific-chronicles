If you've ever enjoyed milk without getting sick or had a vaccine, you owe a HUGE thank you to Louis Pasteur! This French polymath was a biologist, microbiologist, and chemist all rolled into one. He basically proved that tiny microbes cause diseases (germ theory!), gave us pasteurization (goodbye spoiled food!), and pioneered vaccination. He literally saved countless lives and made our food safer. What a legend! 🙏🧪 #GermTheoryHero #PasteurPower #VaccinePioneer
