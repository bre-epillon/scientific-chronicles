On the first night of the 19th century, Giuseppe Piazzi spotted a tiny point of light that didn't stay still. At first, he thought it was a comet, but he soon realized it was a 'dwarf planet' living in the gap between Mars and Jupiter. 

Ceres was the first asteroid ever discovered. For a while, it was considered the eighth planet, until we found a bunch of its friends nearby and had to demote it to 'asteroid.' It was recently promoted back to 'dwarf planet,' proving that even in space, your status can change if you wait long enough. 🪐
