Karl Weierstrass? More like "Karl the WIZARD-strass"! ✨ This German mathematician is often called the "father of modern analysis," and for good reason. He brought a whole new level of rigor and precision to calculus and other foundational math areas. He really tightened up the definitions and proofs, making sure everything was super solid. So, if your math courses are a bit tougher now, you can thank (or blame!) him for making sure everything is *just so*. 😉 Math wouldn't be the same without his foundational work!
