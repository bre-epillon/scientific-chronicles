James Clerk Maxwell? This guy literally brought light (and radio waves, and X-rays...) into focus! 💡 He cooked up Maxwell's equations, a set of brilliant formulas that unified electricity and magnetism, proving they're two sides of the same electromagnetic coin. This wasn't just a neat trick; it showed that light itself is an electromagnetic wave! His work was so foundational, it paved the way for Einstein's relativity and even quantum theory. Talk about a foundational genius! 🌟 What a brain!
