After decades of attempts and several tragedies, a New Zealand beekeeper and a Sherpa mountaineer finally reached the summit of Mount Everest, the highest point on Earth. 

They spent about 15 minutes at the top, took some photos, and ate some mint cake. When they got back down, Hillary reportedly told his friend, 'Well, George, we knocked the bastard off.' It was a triumph of human endurance and teamwork, proving that even the most formidable natural barriers could be conquered. 🏔️
