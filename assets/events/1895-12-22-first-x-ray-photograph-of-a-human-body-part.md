Shortly after discovering X-rays, Wilhelm Röntgen took an image of his wife Anna Bertha’s hand. You can clearly see her bones and her wedding ring. 

When she saw the image, she famously said, 'I have seen my death.' It was a terrifying and thrilling moment. Within months, doctors were using X-rays to find broken bones and bullets. It was the first time we could look inside a living human without a scalpel. It changed medicine overnight. 🦴
