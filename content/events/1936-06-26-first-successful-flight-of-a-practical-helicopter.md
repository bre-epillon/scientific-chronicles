The Focke-Wulf Fw 61 was the first fully controllable helicopter. It could take off vertically, hover, and fly forward, backward, and sideways. 

Before this, 'autogyros' had been around, but they couldn't hover. The Fw 61 proved that vertical flight was a practical reality. It opened up a new world of aviation where you didn't need a runway—just a flat spot of ground. It was the birth of the most versatile vehicle in the sky. 🚁
