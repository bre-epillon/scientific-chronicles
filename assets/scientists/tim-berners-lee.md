Let's be real: you wouldn't be reading this without Tim Berners-Lee! 🙏 This English computer scientist basically invented the World Wide Web. Yeah, *that* Web! He created the system that lets us click links, browse websites, and connect with people all over the globe. Before him, the internet was a bit of a wild west; he gave it structure and made it accessible to *everyone*. So next time you're endlessly scrolling, maybe send a mental thank you his way! 🌐💖
