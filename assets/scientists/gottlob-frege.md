Gottlob Frege might not be a household name, but this guy was basically the superhero of logic! 🤓 He's often called the founder of modern symbolic logic, which sounds complex, but it means he gave us a super precise way to write down and reason about logical statements – like a secret code for thinking! He was obsessed with showing how math isn't just a bunch of numbers but actually an extension of logic itself. If you've ever typed a logical operator in code, you can thank Frege for the foundational ideas. Seriously smart stuff! 🧠
