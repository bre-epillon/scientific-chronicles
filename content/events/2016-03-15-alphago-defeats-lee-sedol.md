The game of Go is much more complex than chess, with more possible board positions than there are atoms in the universe. Scientists thought an AI wouldn't be able to beat a pro for decades. 

But DeepMind's AlphaGo used neural networks and 'reinforcement learning' to teach itself how to play. By defeating 18-time world champion Lee Sedol 4-1, it proved that modern AI could handle 'intuition' and 'creativity.' It was a giant leap for machine learning and a slightly humbling moment for humanity. 碁
