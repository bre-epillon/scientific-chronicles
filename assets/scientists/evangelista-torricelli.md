Ever check the weather and wonder how they know if it's going to rain? You can thank Evangelista Torricelli! 🌬️ This clever Italian physicist and mathematician invented the *barometer*, giving us a way to actually measure atmospheric pressure. Before him, people just thought nature 'abhorred a vacuum,' but Torricelli proved air has weight and pressure. Pretty mind-blowing for the 17th century! On top of that, his advanced work in geometry was a stepping stone for integral calculus. Talk about having a massive impact with a simple device and some brilliant math!
