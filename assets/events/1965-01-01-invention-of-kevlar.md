Stephanie Kwolek was looking for a new, lightweight fiber for tires when she noticed a cloudy, thin solution that most people would have thrown away. 

She insisted on testing it and found that once spun into a fiber, it was incredibly strong and heat-resistant. She had invented Kevlar. It's now used in bulletproof vests, racing sails, and spacecraft. Kwolek’s curiosity has saved thousands of lives. It’s proof that sometimes, the weird-looking stuff in the lab is actually a breakthrough. 🛡️
