Nearly three years after their first flight, the Wright brothers finally received a patent for their 'Flying Machine.' 

Crucially, the patent wasn't just for the plane itself, but for the system of 'wing-warping' that allowed them to control it. They spent much of the next decade in court defending this patent against other pioneers like Glenn Curtiss. It shows that even in the sky, you can't escape the lawyers. ✈️
