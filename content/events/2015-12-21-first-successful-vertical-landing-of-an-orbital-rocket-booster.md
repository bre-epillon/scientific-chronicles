For decades, rockets were disposable—you used them once and threw them away. In 2015, SpaceX changed the game by launching a Falcon 9 and then landing the first stage vertically back at Cape Canaveral. 

It looked like sci-fi come to life. Reusing rockets is the key to making space travel sustainable and affordable. Today, SpaceX lands rockets on ships in the middle of the ocean like it's no big deal. It was the moment the industry realized that the old way of doing things was over. 🚀
