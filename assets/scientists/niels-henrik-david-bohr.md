Niels Bohr completely blew our minds when it came to atoms! Before him, atoms were pretty fuzzy concepts. He came up with a revolutionary model that showed electrons zipping around the nucleus in specific orbits, jumping between energy levels. This was a GIANT leap for quantum theory and totally changed how we picture the tiny world around us. He even snagged a Nobel for it! ⚛️🧪
