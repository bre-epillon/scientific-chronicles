By analyzing magnetic data from the Galileo spacecraft, scientists confirmed that Europa likely has a massive liquid water ocean beneath its icy crust. 

There’s more water on Europa than there is on all of Earth. This discovery made Europa one of the most likely places to find life elsewhere in the solar system. It turns out that 'habitable zones' aren't just about how close you are to the sun; you just need a bit of tidal heating and a lot of ice. 🌊
