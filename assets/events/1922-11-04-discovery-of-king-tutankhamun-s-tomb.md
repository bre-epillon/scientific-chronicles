After years of digging with no luck, Howard Carter was about to give up. Then, his water boy found a step buried in the sand. It led to the intact tomb of a boy king who had been forgotten by history. 

When Carter first looked inside, he was speechless. The tomb was filled with gold, chariots, and the famous golden mask. It sparked a global obsession with ancient Egypt and 'the curse of the pharaohs.' Tutankhamun became more famous in the 20th century than he ever was in the 14th century BC. 👑
