The Three Gorges Dam on the Yangtze River is a project of staggering scale. It’s a mile and a half wide and 600 feet high. 

It generates more hydroelectric power than any other dam on Earth and helps prevent the devastating floods that have plagued the region for centuries. It was controversial, displacing millions of people and submerging historical sites, but it stands as the ultimate example of human planetary engineering in the 21st century. 🌊
