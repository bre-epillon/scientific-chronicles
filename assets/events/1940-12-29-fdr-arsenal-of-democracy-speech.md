In a fireside chat, President Franklin D. Roosevelt called on American industry to transform itself into the 'great arsenal of democracy.' 

This wasn't just a political speech; it was a catalyst for a massive scientific and industrial mobilization. It led to unprecedented breakthroughs in mass production, radar, computing, and medicine. It proved that when science and industry are focused on a single goal, they can move mountains (or at least build 300,000 planes). 🛠️
