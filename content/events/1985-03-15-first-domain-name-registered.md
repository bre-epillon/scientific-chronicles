On March 15, 1985, Symbolics Inc. registered symbolics.com. It was the first '.com' domain ever registered. 

At the time, the internet was a small network for researchers and the military. No one could have predicted that 40 years later, there would be hundreds of millions of domains and that 'dot com' would become synonymous with the global economy. Symbolics is long gone, but their domain remains the '1' in the digital phonebook. 🌐
