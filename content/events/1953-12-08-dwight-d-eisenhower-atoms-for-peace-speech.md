Addressing the UN General Assembly, President Eisenhower proposed the creation of an international agency to promote the peaceful use of nuclear energy. 

He wanted to turn the 'fearful trend of atomic military buildup' into something that could provide medicine, agriculture, and power. This led to the creation of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) and the first commercial nuclear power plants. It was an attempt to find a silver lining in the mushroom cloud. ⚛️
