When ChatGPT was released in late 2022, it felt like the future had suddenly arrived. It wasn't just a chatbot; it was a Large Language Model that could hold nuanced conversations, admit mistakes, and perform complex creative tasks. 

It reached 100 million users in just two months, the fastest growth in tech history. It has sparked a massive debate about the future of work, education, and the definition of creativity. We've officially entered the era of Generative AI, where the line between human and machine output is getting very, very thin. 🤖
