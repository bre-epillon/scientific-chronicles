Okay, so Edward Teller is famously known as 'the father of the H-bomb.' No small feat, that. This Hungarian-American nuclear physicist was at the absolute forefront of developing the world's first thermonuclear weapon. It was a groundbreaking, and frankly, terrifying, leap in destructive power during a very tense era. His work profoundly shaped military strategy and global politics for decades to come. Talk about leaving a mark on history, whether you agree with the outcome or not! ⚛️💥
