In 1947, a young shepherd was looking for a lost goat when he threw a stone into a cave and heard a jar break. Inside were leather scrolls that had been hidden for 2,000 years. 

The Dead Sea Scrolls are some of the oldest surviving biblical texts and provide an incredible window into life in the desert two millennia ago. They survived in the dry air of the Qumran caves, waiting for a stray rock to bring them back to light. It’s the ultimate game of hide and seek. 📜
