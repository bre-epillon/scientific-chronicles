Marie Tharp was a geologist who wasn't allowed on research ships because she was a woman. So, she stayed on land and used the sonar data her colleague Bruce Heezen collected to map the ocean floor. 

She discovered a massive rift valley running down the center of the Atlantic. It was the 'smoking gun' for seafloor spreading and plate tectonics. At first, Heezen dismissed her work as 'girl talk,' but he eventually realized she had found the engine that moves the continents. Tharp literally mapped the hidden face of our planet. 🗺️
