Standing at a dizzying 2,717 feet, the Burj Khalifa is more than twice as tall as the Empire State Building. It’s a vertical city made of glass, steel, and concrete. 

To build it, engineers had to solve incredible challenges, like how to pump concrete 1,900 feet straight up and how to keep the building from swaying too much in the desert winds. It features the world's highest nightclub, the world's highest restaurant, and probably the world's most stressed-out window washers. It is the ultimate monument to human ambition. 🏙️
