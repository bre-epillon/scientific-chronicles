After Sputnik, the US was under pressure to get into the game. Explorer 1 was their first successful attempt. Unlike Sputnik, it carried scientific instruments. 

Led by James Van Allen, the mission discovered the Van Allen radiation belts—bands of charged particles trapped by Earth's magnetic field. It was the first major scientific discovery of the space age, proving that space wasn't just empty; it was full of invisible, high-energy drama. 🚀
