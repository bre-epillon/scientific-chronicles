Jawed Karim, one of the co-founders of YouTube, uploaded a low-res video of himself talking about elephant trunks. It wasn't profound, but it was the start of a platform that would change media forever. 

YouTube democratized video, giving everyone a global broadcast channel. Today, over 500 hours of video are uploaded every minute. From makeup tutorials to revolutions, it’s all been caught on camera and shared with the world. All because of a guy and some elephants. 🐘
