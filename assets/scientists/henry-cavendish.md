Henry Cavendish was one of those old-school natural philosophers who was basically good at *everything*. 🎩 This English genius not only discovered hydrogen – yep, that's right, he identified the element we now know as H! – but he also figured out how to 'weigh' the Earth! His famous Cavendish experiment was a super precise way to measure the planet's density. Imagine doing that without modern tech! 🤯 He was a true scientific pioneer, making huge strides in chemistry and physics without needing a spotlight.
