Wilhelm Röntgen was messing around with vacuum tubes in a dark lab when he noticed a nearby screen glowing. He soon realized that some invisible rays were passing through solid objects—including his own skin. 

Naturally, the first thing he did was ask his wife, Anna Bertha, to put her hand in the path of the rays. When she saw the image of her own bones and wedding ring, she reportedly exclaimed, 'I have seen my death!' Which, honestly, is a fair reaction to seeing your own skeleton for the first time. Physics: terrifying people since 1895. 🦴
