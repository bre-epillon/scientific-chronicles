📌 August 8, 1902.

🇬🇧On this day, Paul Adrien Maurice Dirac was born. He was an English theoretical physicist regarded as (one of) the most infuential physicists of the 20th century.
Dirac made fundamental contributions to the early development of both quantum mechanics and quantum electrodynamics. 
Among other discoveries, he formulated the Dirac equation which describes the behaviour of fermions and predicted the existence of antimatter. Dirac shared the 1933 Nobel Prize in Physics with Erwin Schrödinger "for the discovery of new productive forms of atomic theory". He also made significant contributions to the reconciliation of the theory of relativity with quantum mechanics.
