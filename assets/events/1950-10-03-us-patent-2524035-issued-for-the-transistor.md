Three years after the first successful test, Bell Labs was granted the patent for the Point-Contact Transistor. 

This document represents the formal beginning of the semiconductor revolution. It marked the end of the vacuum tube era and the start of the digital age. It's the reason we have computers in our pockets today instead of rooms full of glowing glass tubes. A small patent for a small device that did a very large thing. ⚡
