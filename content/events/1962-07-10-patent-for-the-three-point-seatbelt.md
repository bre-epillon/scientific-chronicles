Before Nils Bohlin, seatbelts were simple lap straps that often did more harm than good in a crash. Bohlin, a former aviation engineer at Volvo, realized that you needed to secure both the upper and lower body. 

He designed the three-point belt, which is easy to use and incredibly effective. Volvo, in a legendary move, left the patent open so every car manufacturer could use it for free. It is arguably the most important safety invention in history. Click it or ticket—but mostly, just click it to stay alive. 🚗
