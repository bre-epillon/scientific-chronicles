James Cameron didn't just direct movies about the deep; he built a submarine called the *Deepsea Challenger* and took it to the Challenger Deep, 35,787 feet down. 

He was the first person to reach the bottom solo and the first to visit since 1960. He spent several hours collecting samples and filming in 3D. It was a massive technical achievement that combined Hollywood production with serious deep-sea science. It turns out the bottom of the ocean is just as alien as anything on Pandora. 🌊
