Okay, let's talk about Enrico Fermi! This Italian physicist was an absolute powerhouse. He wasn't just *part* of history; he made it, big time! He created Chicago Pile-1, the world's *first* self-sustaining nuclear reactor. Think about that for a second – he literally kicked off the nuclear age! ⚛️ He was also a key figure in developing the atomic bomb during WWII. And before all that, he was already a Nobel laureate, snagging the Physics prize in 1938 for his work on induced radioactivity. Seriously, this guy was operating on another level!
