Artemis 1 was the first flight of the Space Launch System (SLS), the most powerful rocket ever built, and the Orion spacecraft. It was an uncrewed test flight that took Orion further than any spacecraft built for humans has ever gone. 

It’s the start of a program to put the first woman and the first person of color on the Moon. We're not just visiting this time; we're staying. Artemis is the first step toward a permanent lunar base and the eventual journey to Mars. The Moon is calling, and we're finally picking up. 🌕
