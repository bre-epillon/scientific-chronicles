What makes magnets tick? Louis Néel, a French physicist, spent his career figuring that out! 🧲 He shared the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1970 for his pioneering work on the magnetic properties of solids. He essentially explained why some materials are super magnetic, some are weakly magnetic, and some are just plain weird. His theories on antiferromagnetism and ferrimagnetism were game-changers for understanding materials science. He magnetized our world! #Physics #Magnetism #NobelLaureate
