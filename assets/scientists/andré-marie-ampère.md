So, electricity and magnetism? While Ørsted connected them, André-Marie Ampère basically *defined* the whole field! This French polymath didn't just dabble; he founded and named the science of electrodynamics (what we now call electromagnetism). He figured out how electric currents interact and even how to quantify it. No wonder the unit of electric current, the 'ampere,' is named after him! ⚡💪
