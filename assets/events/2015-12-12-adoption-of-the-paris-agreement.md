In 2015, 196 parties at COP21 in Paris signed a landmark agreement to limit global warming to well below 2 degrees Celsius. Unlike Kyoto, the Paris Agreement included commitments from both developed and developing nations. 

It was a historic moment of global consensus. Each country set its own targets, creating a flexible but unified framework for saving the biosphere. It’s the ultimate group project, and the stakes couldn't be higher. Let's hope we all do our homework. 🌍
