Michael Faraday was a self-taught genius who realized that moving a magnet through a coil of wire creates an electric current. 

This simple discovery is the basis for almost every way we generate power today, from wind turbines to nuclear plants. It also gave us the electric motor. When asked what use his discovery was, Faraday reportedly asked, 'What use is a newborn baby?' (Yes, scientists use that line a lot). He had just fathered the modern electrical world. ⚡
