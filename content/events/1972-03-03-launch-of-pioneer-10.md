Pioneer 10 was the first true deep-space explorer. It was the first spacecraft to travel through the asteroid belt (proving it wasn't a wall of death) and the first to get a close look at Jupiter. 

On board is a famous plaque designed by Carl Sagan, showing humans and our location in the galaxy, just in case any extraterrestrials find it. It's currently billions of miles away, heading toward the star Aldebaran. It'll get there in about 2 million years. Hope they like our drawings. 🛰️
