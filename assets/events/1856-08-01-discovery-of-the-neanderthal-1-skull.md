In the Neander Valley, miners found some old bones in a limestone cave. They thought they belonged to a bear, but a local teacher realized they looked like a human—just... different. 

It was the first evidence of another species of human that had lived on Earth alongside us. At first, people thought it was just a diseased modern human, but we eventually realized it was our closest relative. It was a humbling reminder that we haven't always been the only ones in the family tree. 💀
