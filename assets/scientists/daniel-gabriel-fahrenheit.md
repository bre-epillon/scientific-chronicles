Ever checked the weather in Fahrenheit? You've got Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit to thank for that! 🌡️ This guy was a total pioneer in making thermometers accurate. He invented the super precise mercury-in-glass thermometer and, of course, gave us his very own temperature scale. Before him, guessing the temperature was practically a sport. He brought science to keeping things cool (or hot)! 🔥
