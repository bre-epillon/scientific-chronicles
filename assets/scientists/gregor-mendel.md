Meet Gregor Mendel, the OG geneticist! 🌱 This Austrian monk spent his days messing around with pea plants (way more exciting than it sounds, trust us!). He figured out how traits get passed down from one generation to the next, basically giving us the ABCs of heredity. His "Laws of Heredity" laid the mathematical groundwork for genetics. Who knew peas held so many secrets? 🤯
