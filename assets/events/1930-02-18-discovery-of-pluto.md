Clyde Tombaugh was a 24-year-old farm boy turned astronomer who spent months comparing photos of the sky. He was looking for 'Planet X,' a ninth planet predicted to exist beyond Neptune. 

In February 1930, he found it. Pluto became a beloved member of the solar system family for 76 years, until it was controversialy 'reclassified' in 2006. Regardless of its title, Pluto remains the king of the Kuiper Belt and a favorite of everyone who loves a subatomic underdog. 🧊
