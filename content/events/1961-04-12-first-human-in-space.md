In 1961, Yuri Gagarin squeezed into the tiny Vostok 1 capsule and became the first human to orbit the Earth. As he blasted off, he famously shouted 'Poyekhali!' (Let's go!). 

Gagarin's flight lasted less than two hours, but it shattered the psychological barrier of what humans could achieve. He returned a global hero, proving that a person could survive the vacuum of space and the heat of reentry. The door to the stars was officially open. 👨‍🚀
