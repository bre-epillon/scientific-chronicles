ELIZA was the world's first chatbot. Her most famous script, DOCTOR, would parody a Rogerian therapist by reflecting the user's questions back at them. 

User: 'I'm sad.' ELIZA: 'Why do you say you are sad?' Despite being a very simple program, people found themselves becoming emotionally attached to ELIZA. It was the first time we realized how easily the human mind can be fooled into thinking a computer has 'empathy.' It was a warning from the 60s that we're still grappling with today. 🤖
