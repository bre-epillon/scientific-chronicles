Abdus Salam was a Pakistani-British physicist who made the universe a little less mysterious! 🌌 He snagged a Nobel Prize in 1979 for a groundbreaking theory that showed two of the universe's fundamental forces – the weak nuclear force and the electromagnetic force – are actually two sides of the same coin. It's called electroweak unification, and it was a huge step toward a "theory of everything." Seriously big brain stuff! #Physics #NobelPrize #Electroweak
