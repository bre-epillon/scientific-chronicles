For thousands of years, smallpox killed, blinded, and scarred millions. But through a massive, global vaccination effort, the world managed to corner the virus and wipe it out. 

On December 8, 1979, the World Health Organization officially declared the world free of smallpox. It is the only human disease we have ever completely eradicated. It remains the greatest achievement of international cooperation in history. Smallpox: 0, Humanity: 1. 🌍
