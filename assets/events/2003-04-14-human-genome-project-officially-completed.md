After 13 years and billions of dollars, an international team of scientists finally mapped out all 3 billion letters of the human DNA code. 

It was like discovering the periodic table for the human body. The Human Genome Project has transformed how we understand disease, ancestry, and what it means to be human. We finally have the blueprints; now we just have to figure out how to read all the footnotes. It’s the ultimate autobiography of our species. 🧬
