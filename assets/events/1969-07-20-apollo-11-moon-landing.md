On July 20, 1969, the Eagle landed at Tranquility Base. Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin became the first humans to set foot on another world, while Michael Collins kept the 'getaway car' running in orbit. 

Armstrong’s 'one small step' was watched by an estimated 650 million people. It remains the pinnacle of human exploration and technological achievement. We proved that we could leave our cradle and touch the heavens. And yes, they brought back rocks. Lots of very expensive rocks. 🌕
