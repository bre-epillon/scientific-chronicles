Christian Goldbach left us with one of math's coolest (and toughest!) puzzles: Goldbach's Conjecture. This guy basically asked, 'Is every even number greater than 2 the sum of two prime numbers?' Sounds simple, right? Centuries later, mathematicians are still trying to prove it! What a challenge to leave behind! 🧠🔢
