Ever thought about how much energy a car uses or how efficient a power plant is? You can thank Nicolas-Léonard-Sadi Carnot for laying down the law on that! 🔥 This French engineer and physicist basically wrote the rulebook for thermodynamics, especially when it came to heat engines. He figured out the *absolute maximum* efficiency an engine could ever achieve. Super practical insights that still power our world today! 💪
