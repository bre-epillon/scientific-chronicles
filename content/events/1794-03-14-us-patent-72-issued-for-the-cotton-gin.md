In 1794, Eli Whitney was granted a patent for the cotton gin, a simple machine that could quickly separate cotton fibers from their seeds. 

While the machine was a brilliant piece of engineering, it had massive and unintended consequences. It made cotton highly profitable, which unfortunately led to the expansion of slavery in the American South. It shows that technology doesn't exist in a vacuum; it can reshape societies in ways the inventor never intended. ⚙️
