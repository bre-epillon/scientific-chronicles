When scientists discovered that CFCs were destroying the ozone layer, the world actually did something about it. The Montreal Protocol is an international treaty designed to phase out the production of ozone-depleting substances. 

It is widely considered the most successful environmental agreement in history. Every single country in the UN has ratified it. Thanks to this treaty, the ozone layer is expected to fully recover by the middle of this century. It proves that when the threat is clear, we are capable of saving our own skins. 🌍
