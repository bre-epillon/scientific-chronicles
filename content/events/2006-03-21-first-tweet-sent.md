In 2006, Jack Dorsey sent the first message on a new platform: 'just setting up my twttr.' It was a simple status update in 140 characters or less. 

Twitter became the world's 'real-time' town square, changing how news is broken and how we interact with celebrities and politicians. It proved that in the age of information overload, brevity is king. Love it or hate it, those 140 characters have shaped the 21st-century conversation. 🐦
